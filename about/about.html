Glossary

Effect
A magic trick, what the spectors see but is not real.
Gimmick
An extra tool that a magician uses in the trick but the spectators don't see.
Penetration
The effect that a solid object goes through another solid object.
Sleight of Hand
Magic skills that does not requires the help of gimmicks.
